Synthesizing the tactile sensation through transcutaneous electrical stimulation by targeting the periodontal ligament

My VR project explores simulating food textures through electrical stimulation by targeting periodontal ligament. Leveraging 3D printing for electrode placement enhanced the approach, uncovering that targeted stimulation techniques might offer precise tactile sensations.
